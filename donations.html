Are you interested in becoming a part of the change? Do you wish to help Advocacy Outreach educate and assist those who have less opportunity to become more self-sufficient?

Advocacy Outreach has been building successful people and families with the resources to take care of their own needs, both immediate and long-term, and the ability to advocate for themselves in the community, schools and the workplace since 1992. Our Mission is to facilitate social change and justice through advocacy and a provision of services to the poor and disenfranchised in Bastrop County and surrounding rural areas.

Be a part of this action!! Your generous donations will enable us to prevent or overcome homelessness or provide assistance linking to resources people need to increase financial stability, address health needs or personal crises. Your donations will help Advocacy Outreach to continue and expand educational services to the community through family literacy programs that address the literacy needs of parents and their preschool-age children, ESL education, GED education, job and career preparation. Your donations help us provide assistance to people needing help in understanding forms, regulations and other legal requirements.
