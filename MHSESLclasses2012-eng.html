Information About Evening English Classes for Adults
~ Manor High School

Welcome to English classes at Manor ISD! Many students like you have attended our classes and have been successful towards meeting their goals. Classes will be offered for Beginning and Intermediate ESL students only.

Due to the large number of students interested in English classes, we cannot take walk-ins. Please attend the next orientation scheduled for Monday, October 15, at Manor High School. All new and returning students must attend an orientation before they may enroll in a class.

Date: October 15, 2012
Time: 5:30 pm
Place: Manor High School 9th Grace Center

Classes are 2 evenings per week, 6 pm to 8 pm. Child care will be available for children ages 3-10 years old only. No babies or toddlers, please!

At orientation, students will learn more about the classes, fill out the registration form and schedule a time to take an English assessment. The assessment will help to determine which class is right for you.

Returning students must also attend the registration on October 15th. Students with a posttest from a previous year will be assigned a class at that time. Students without a posttest will be placed on a waiting list to return to classes.

In order to be successful, it is very important that you agree to attend classes regularly. Students are required to attend 60 or more hours of classes during the school year. If you have to miss classes or make schedule changes, please let your teacher know and make arrangements to attend another class. If you cannot attend at least 75% of the class time each month, you will be put on a waiting list to return to class.

We hope to see you on October 15th at Manor High School!!
