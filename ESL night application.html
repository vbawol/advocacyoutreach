Advocacy Outreach es una 501 (c) (3) organización que ha recibido una beca de la Fundación Barbara Bush para la Educación Familiar para proporcionar servicios en idioma Inglés de alfabetización para padres y familias. Advocacy Outreach is a 501 (c) (3) organization that utilizes a grant from the Barbara Bush Foundation for Family Literacy to provide services in English language literacy for parents and families.  Advocacy Outreach and Manor ISD work together to provide these services as well as evening English classes for adults.
