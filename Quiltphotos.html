Beautiful Handmade Quilts Donated to Advocacy Outreach by the Piece by Piece Quilters

Over many years, the wonderful ladies of Piece by Piece quilting group have created and donated many quilts for the children in our early childhood development center.

Each quilt is unique, made by hand and of exellent quality and craftmanship. Some years they also made book bags and various hand bags.
